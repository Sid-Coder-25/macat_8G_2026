Chapter V

Main Theories

The leading clues in the search for Alexander.

Strongest Theory

Alexandria

The strongest theory. Ancient records place the tomb there, close to the heart of the city Alexander founded.

Popular Clue

Nabi Daniel Mosque Area

A frequently discussed location in modern Alexandria, but no proof has yet confirmed the tomb beneath it.

Dramatic Possibility

Siwa Oasis

A striking theory tied to Alexander's visit to the oracle, though the historical case remains uncertain.

Least Likely

Greece

His homeland still inspires speculation, but ancient accounts point much more strongly toward Egypt.
