Chapter VI

Why It Matters

A discovery that could reshape ancient history.

One of history's greatest possible discoveries

Finding the tomb would be one of history's greatest discoveries. It could reveal how Alexander was buried, how he was remembered, and how his legend survived after death.

It would also connect written history with physical evidence, giving the modern world a direct link to one of antiquity's most famous figures.

Final Reflection

The empire fell. The city changed.

The empire fell. The city changed. But somewhere, Alexander may still be waiting to be found.
